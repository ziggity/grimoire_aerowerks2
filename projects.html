Our Projects

We started our journey with the iconic, timeless E36 BMW platform, transforming it into a full carbon fiber widebody SEMA build. This ambitious project showcases our dedication to high-quality, lightweight, and visually striking carbon fiber components for cars that deserve a place in automotive culture.

Corvette C6 Drift Car

As our reputation grew, so did demand for our expertise. We’re excited to introduce our latest project: a Corvette C6 drift car constructed from full carbon aramid Kevlar. This build pushes the boundaries of performance and style, combining the power of the Corvette with cutting-edge carbon fiber technology – and making a statement on the track.

Our Commitment

At Grimoire Aerowerks, we’re committed to projects that embody speed, strength, and precision. From custom car parts to diverse applications like golf clubs, money clips, and medical devices, we craft innovative solutions using composite materials. Our products are not only strong and lightweight but also corrosion-resistant and engineered for speed.
