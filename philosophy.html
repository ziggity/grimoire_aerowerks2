Grimoire Aerowerks' Philosophy

We’re not here to mess around. We craft automotive components that blend classic aesthetics with cutting-edge technology. Grimoire Aerowerks simply wants real carbon fiber to go on real applied cars. Whether it be for your track car, restomod, or show car, we respect the science and beauty of aerodynamics and its materials necessary to achieve this.

Shop Products Projects

We’re Committed to:

Real

No fiberglass. We utilize only genuine carbon fiber to deliver exceptional strength, weight reduction, and a stunning visual appeal. None of that fake carbon BS which everyone claims is carbon.

Precision

Our skilled artisans meticulously craft each component, ensuring precise fitment and impeccable quality. We take our time. We take pride.

Performance

Our carbon fiber parts are engineered to enhance the performance of your vehicle, whether it’s on the track or the street. Weigh another component against ours - you’ll feel the difference.

Experience the lightness. Experience the strength. Explore our range of carbon fiber products and transform your classic car into a modern masterpiece.
